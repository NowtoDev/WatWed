Wat Chedi Luang Worawihan

Wat Chedi Luang Worawihan is an ancient temple located in the heart of Chiang Mai, home to the largest chedi (stupa) in the province. The temple also houses the sacred Inthakhin Pillar, the city pillar that symbolizes stability and prosperity for the people of Chiang Mai. Visitors who have the chance to participate in the traditional *Khan Dok* offering ceremony, water-pouring rituals, and pay homage to Phra Chao Fon Saen Ha (the Rain King Buddha) at various auspicious spots around the temple will receive blessings and good fortune.
